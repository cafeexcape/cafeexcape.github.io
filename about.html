My Story: Passion, Artistry & Dedication

Crafting timeless beauty, one gem at a time, with a heart full of passion.

My Journey into Jewelry

kuzuzangpo! I'm Leki Choden, the hands and heart behind Leki Choden Jewelry. My fascination with precious stones and the intricate art of jewelry making began years ago. What started as a hobby blossomed into a profound passion, driving me to master the skills required to transform raw materials into wearable art.

Every piece I create is a reflection of my dedication to craftsmanship and my belief in the power of jewelry to express individuality and capture cherished moments. From selecting the finest gems to meticulously setting each stone, I oversee every step with precision and love.

I am deeply passionate about bringing unique and beautiful jewelry to the wonderful people of Phuentsholing and beyond. My goal is to create pieces that not only adorn but also tell a story, a story of elegance, quality, and timeless appeal.

Inspired by Excellence

Louis-François Cartier

My work draws significant inspiration from the legendary Louis-François Cartier. His pioneering spirit, relentless pursuit of excellence, and revolutionary approach to jewelry design laid the groundwork for modern luxury. I aim to infuse that same spirit of innovation and timeless elegance into my own creations, respecting the heritage of fine jewelry while adding my unique contemporary touch.

Bhutanese Heritage

Living in the serene and culturally rich landscape of Phuentsholing, Bhutan, also deeply influences my artistic vision. The vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and peaceful philosophy of Bhutanese art often find their way into my designs, creating a harmonious blend of traditional aesthetics and modern sophistication.

The Beauty of Love

At the core of my inspiration is love itself – the love for the craft, the love between people, and the love for beauty. I believe jewelry is a powerful symbol of affection, commitment, and personal expression. Each piece is designed to be cherished, a tangible representation of emotions and memories.
